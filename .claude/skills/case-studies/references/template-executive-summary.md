**Format:** Single page (PDF or print)
**Target length:** ~400-500 words

### Design Tips

- **Primary metric**: Make this the largest number on the page (use 36pt+ font)
- **Quote block**: Use a distinct background color or border
- **Logos**: Customer logo only if branded; [Company] logo in header/footer
- **White space**: Don't crowd—let key numbers and quotes breathe
- **Color accent**: Use brand color for metric highlights

### When to Use

- **Sales calls**: Leave-behind after demos or discovery calls
- **Email attachments**: Quick summary for prospects who want the highlights
- **Internal briefings**: Share wins with leadership or cross-functional teams
- **Events**: Printed handouts at conferences or trade shows
