## Platform-Specific Notes

**LinkedIn:**
- Long-form posts perform well
- Include 3-5 hashtags at the end
- Tag the customer company if branded and they've approved

**Twitter/X:**
- Threads get more engagement than single tweets for case studies
- Use the thread format for full story, single tweet for quick hits
- 2-3 hashtags max

**Quote Graphics:**
- 1:1 ratio works across platforms
- Ensure text is readable on mobile
- Include branding subtly
