# Case Study Interview Questions

Use these questions when interviewing customers or reviewing call recordings. Not all questions apply to every case study—select based on what information gaps exist.

## Opening / Background

- Can you briefly describe your role and what your team is responsible for?
- What does your tech stack look like? (Kubernetes, cloud provider, key services)
- How many services/microservices are you running?

## Before [Company]

- What was your environment and approach to security before [Company]?
- How were you handling threat detection? What tools were you using?
- What gaps or challenges existed with that approach?
- What was the biggest pain point for your team?
- How long did it typically take to debug a production issue?
- What was the business impact of those gaps? Impact on your team? On your customers?

## Why They Chose [Company]

- What made you look for a new solution?
- Did you evaluate other solutions? What didn't work about them?
- Why did you choose [Company] specifically?
- What stood out compared to alternatives?
- Who was involved in the decision? What were their concerns?

## After [Company]

- What changed after [Company] was in place?
- Can you quantify the improvement? (MTTR, time savings, coverage %)
- How has it affected your team's workflow day-to-day?
- Were there business results you can point to?
- Any unexpected benefits you didn't anticipate?
- Has it improved anything for your own customers or end users?

## Key Results (Probe for Specifics)

- What are the top 2-3 metrics that improved?
- Can you give me before/after numbers?
- Any cost savings or efficiency gains you can quantify?

## Quotable Moments

- If you had to describe the change to a peer at another company, what would you say?
- What would you tell someone considering [Company]?

## Logistics

- Are you comfortable being named, or do you prefer anonymity?
- Who needs to approve the final case study on your side?
- Any metrics or details that are off-limits?
