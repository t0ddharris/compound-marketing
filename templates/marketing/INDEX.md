# Marketing Output Index

All content generated by the harness lives here. Organized by type.

## Structure

- [[plans/]] — Backlog of improvements, new skills, tooling ideas, and project plans

## How Content Lands Here

When a skill or agent produces output (blog post, social post, case study, report), the finished file goes in this directory. Files are named with a date prefix and descriptive slug: `2026-05-14-blog-runtime-detection.md`.

## Finding Content

Browse by folder, or search by keyword. As content accumulates, subfolders will be added by type:
- `blogs/` — Blog post drafts and finals
- `social/` — Social media posts by platform
- `case-studies/` — Customer case studies
- `emails/` — Email sequences and templates
- `reports/` — Audits, analyses, performance reviews
- `campaigns/` — Multi-channel campaign materials
