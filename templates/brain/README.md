# Marketing Brain

This folder is the single source of truth for all marketing content.

## Human Authority

The repository owner is the final authority on all product facts, positioning, and messaging. All additions to `/brain/` require their approval.

## Ownership

- **Product Marketer** owns and maintains all files in `/brain/`
- All other agents may **read** but **not modify** these files
- The repository owner has final approval over all content

## Rules

1. All factual claims must originate from `truth.md`
2. If information is missing, agents must ask or mark `[VERIFY]`
3. No agent may invent product facts, customer names, metrics, or features
4. When in doubt, leave a placeholder rather than guess

---

## File Purposes

### Source of Truth

| File | Purpose | Usage |
|------|---------|-------|
| `truth.md` | Approved facts allowlist (company, product, customers) | **Only source** of factual claims |

### Strategy & Messaging

| File | Purpose | Usage |
|------|---------|-------|
| `positioning-and-messaging.md` | Combined positioning, ICP, competitive, and messaging | Section 1 = internal strategy; Section 2 = external-safe messaging |
| `competitive.md` | Competitive landscape, territory map, differentiation themes | Competitive content and battlecards |
| `personas.md` | Buyer persona profiles | Content targeting and sales enablement |

### Market Evidence

| File | Purpose | Usage |
|------|---------|-------|
| `market-signals.md` | Industry news, incidents, and trends that validate your thesis | Consumed by content, social, sales narratives |
| `audience-language.md` | How buyers talk about the problem | Language calibration for all content |
| `customer-journey.md` | How prospects find you, convert, stall, and expand | Email sequences, landing pages, sales enablement |

### Product

| File | Purpose | Usage |
|------|---------|-------|
| `capabilities.md` | Product features and technical capabilities | Datasheets, technical content, competitive comparisons |
| `use-cases.md` | Canonical use cases and proof library | Content, sales, campaigns |
| `qualifying-questions.md` | Discovery and qualification questions | Sales enablement |

### Downstream / Derivative

| File | Purpose | Usage |
|------|---------|-------|
| `tactical-assets.md` | Boilerplate, pitches, snippets | Ready-to-use execution assets |

---

## Key Distinctions

- **`positioning-and-messaging.md` Section 1** is primarily internal, covering market truths, ICP, competitive POV
- **`positioning-and-messaging.md` Section 2** is the source for external-ready language; public content derives from here
- **`use-cases.md`** and **`tactical-assets.md`** are downstream; they consume from messaging and truth
- **`truth.md`** is the source of factual claims; other files reference it
